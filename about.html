Playa Sol Dive Shop

Diving for over 20 years, Tom Walsh decided to open up Playa Sol Diving along the beautiful Mayan Riviera of Mexico.
We have guest dive instructors from all over the world and offer our dives in four different languages - Spanish, English, French and Italian

All of our divemasters, be it guest, or one of our two full time instrcuturs, are all Padi accreditted
with at least 10 years of experience each.
We have won tripadvisor awards for the past 5 years in our sister dive shops in Tulum
so rest assured you are in the best hands with us!

When diving with us, be it your first dive or one-hundredth you can be sure that your safety will be our top priority.
Each dive comes with full equipment, so you only need to worry about bringing yourself along!
